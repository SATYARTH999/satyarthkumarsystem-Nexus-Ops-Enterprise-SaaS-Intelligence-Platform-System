## Overview

# NexusOps: Enterprise SaaS Analytics Platform

A modern, high-fidelity UI/UX design project for a multi-tenant B2B Enterprise SaaS Analytics Platform.

The platform helps businesses track complex KPIs, manage revenue, and streamline operations through role-based access control and customizable widgets.

## Features

- **Role-Based Dashboards:** Custom views for Admins, Managers, and Analysts.
- **Customizable Widgets:** Drag-and-drop canvas to rearrange performance metrics.
- **Data Visualization:** Clean charts, retention heat maps, and operational trends.
- **Advanced Filtering:** Segment enterprise data by date, region, and product tiers.
- **Access Control (RBAC):** Granular permission settings and team directories.
- **Billing & Revenue:** Subscription breakdown, transaction histories, and ledgers.

---

## Design System

- **Type:** Desktop Web Application (1440px width)
- **Colors:** White background, neutral gray borders, deep enterprise blue, and success green.
- **Focus:** High information density balanced with clean, scannable white space.
